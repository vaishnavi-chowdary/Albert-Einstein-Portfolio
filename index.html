Hello, I'm Albert Einstein

Physicist | Humanitarian | Inventor


I am best known for developing the theory of relativity, one of the pillars of modern physics. I also made significant contributions to quantum mechanics, cosmology, and other fields of science. I am also passionate about social justice, human rights, and pacifism. I advocated for the creation of a world government, the protection of civil liberties, and the prevention of nuclear war. I have a sense of humor and a love for life.
